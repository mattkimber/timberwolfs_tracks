Signal scheme:

Block: red
Entry: yellow
Combo: red + yellow
Exit: red + red

Path: --| --|
        |   |
         \ /
          |

Path (one way): as path, with board



Colour

Block: 4 light
Entry: 4 light (single yellow) 
Combo: 4 light (double yellow)
Exit:  4 light

Path: 4 light + direction indicator
Path/1way: 4 light + direction indicator + board

(4th rail/light rail use 2 light signals)
